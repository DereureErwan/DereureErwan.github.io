Erwan Dereure

Postdoctoral Scientist · Institut de Biologie de l'École Normale Supérieure, Paris · Computational Biology and Applied Mathematics Unit

I am currently looking for a PostDoc position in Image Processing and Applied Mathematics, with a particular interest in Biomedical Imaging and Remote Sensing.

I completed my PhD in Biomedical Image Analysis under the supervision of Christophe Kervazo, Elsa Angelini ( IMAGES group, Télécom Paris) and Jean-Christophe Olivo-Marin ( Biological Image Analysis Unit, Institut Pasteur). My thesis focused on optimization-based methods for Blind Source Separation applied to Bioluminescent Imaging and on strengthening the robustness of quantification to improve experimental protocols.
